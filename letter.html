To My Raha ❤️

From Zyan

My Love, If you're reading this, thank you for giving me a little of your time. I know I hurt you by forgetting to tell you that I was going out to play. To someone else it may seem like a small mistake, but I understand why it wasn't small to you. It wasn't about me going out. It was about you feeling like I forgot to include you. And for that... I'm truly sorry. You deserve someone who makes you feel important in every little moment. You deserve someone who tells you where they're going, not because they have to... But because they want you to feel safe and valued. I can't change what happened. But I can promise you something. From today... I'll communicate better. I'll remember the little things. I'll never let carelessness make you question your place in my life. Because your place is right here... In my heart. I don't expect you to forgive me immediately. I only hope that with time, my actions will prove how serious I am. Thank you for loving me. Thank you for caring enough to be upset. Because if you didn't care... You wouldn't have been hurt. I love you, Raha. Always. — Zyan ❤️
